Plan Recommendation Wizard

Business Problem

A 30% time cost to the sales team in talking to new users and recommending the right product configuration for them.

Solution

A feature that asks the user a series of questions to understand the nature of their business and uses their responses to offer a recommendation for what plan would be best suited to their needs.

Impact

The time spent by the sales team in performing this task dropped down to around 15%.
